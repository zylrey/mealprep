7-Day Meal Prep Plan

Meal Plan Overview

  • Breakfast: Protein + veggies (light but filling)
  • Lunch: Hearty Filipino-inspired dish + yellow corn rice
  • Dinner: Light, protein-based meal + yellow corn rice
  • Snacks: Low-calorie options (fruits, boiled eggs)

Day 1

Breakfast: Scrambled eggs with tomatoes (2 eggs, 1 chopped tomato), ½ cup yellow corn rice

Lunch: Grilled chicken inasal, 1 cup yellow corn rice, steamed kangkong with bagoong

Dinner: Tinolang manok, ½ cup yellow corn rice

Day 2

Breakfast: Tuna omelette (1 can tuna in water, 2 eggs), ½ cup yellow corn rice

Lunch: Pork adobo, 1 cup yellow corn rice, blanched string beans

Dinner: Bangus steak, ½ cup yellow corn rice, cucumber salad

Day 3

Breakfast: Boiled egg (2 eggs), sautéed spinach with garlic, ½ cup yellow corn rice

Lunch: Bistek Tagalog, 1 cup yellow corn rice, steamed broccoli

Dinner: Tortang talong, ½ cup yellow corn rice, ensaladang talong

Day 4

Breakfast: Garlic fried yellow corn rice (½ cup), fried egg (1 piece), sliced tomatoes on the side

Lunch: Sinigang na baboy, 1 cup yellow corn rice

Dinner: Grilled tilapia, ½ cup yellow corn rice, chopped salted egg with tomatoes

Day 5

Breakfast: Sautéed sardines (1 small can), ½ cup yellow corn rice

Lunch: Chicken curry, 1 cup yellow corn rice, steamed pechay

Dinner: Grilled tofu and eggplant, ½ cup yellow corn rice, side of atsara

Day 6

Breakfast: Longganisa (2 pieces), ½ cup yellow corn rice, sliced cucumber on the side

Lunch: Ginisang monggo, 1 cup yellow corn rice

Dinner: Pinakbet, ½ cup yellow corn rice

Day 7

Breakfast: Boiled saba banana (1 piece), fried egg (1 piece), ½ cup yellow corn rice

Lunch: Chicken afritada, 1 cup yellow corn rice

Dinner: Sautéed ampalaya with egg, ½ cup yellow corn rice, grilled squid

Snack Ideas

  • Boiled saba banana
  • Boiled eggs
  • Fresh fruits (apple, papaya, watermelon)
  • Plain yogurt
  • Small handful of nuts

Tips for Meal Prep

  1. Batch cook yellow corn rice: Cook a large batch and store it in portions for easy reheating.
  2. Prep vegetables: Wash and chop veggies ahead of time to cut down on cooking time.
  3. Marinate proteins: Pre-marinate meats like chicken inasal or pork adobo for more flavor and convenience.
